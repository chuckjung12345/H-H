🔐 Security Update – What You Need to Know

✅ Summary of Key Changes (Effective August 1st)

  • 👤 Only members will be issued a Clear Pass.
  • 👷️ All contractors must check in at the main gate for every visit.
  • 📲 Add your vendors/guests to the gate access app or QR code now.
  • 🏨 Open houses: Guests must park at the admin lot and be escorted by a realtor.
  • ❌ All contractor passes will be deactivated August 1st.
  • 🔗 Download the Clear Pass Application

🤭 Why These Changes Are Happening

Our current contractor pass system:

  • Allows access after projects are completed.
  • Often skips identity checks.
  • Creates serious security risks.

Mariner Sands is shifting to a resident-focused access model to better protect the community.

🏗️ About the New Clear Pass Policy

  • This system was introduced to ease front gate congestion.
  • Outdated contractor passes and misuse have undermined security.
  • All entry points will now require clear passes.

📣 What You Can Do Right Now

  • Review and Update Access Lists – Remove old vendors and inactive guests.
  • Use the ABDI App – Enable faster access and call in guests ahead of time.
  • Notify Vendors – Let them know Clear Pass is required and gate check-in is mandatory.

🚪 Special Rules for Open Houses

  • Realtors must escort prospective buyers.
  • Buyers must park in the admin lot.
  • No unsupervised access to amenities or property.

💬 Final Note

We understand change can be inconvenient. But these updates are part of a larger effort to ensure safety, privacy, and peace of mind for all members.

Thank you for your cooperation.
