BOG Recommendation – Responsive Design Policy

  • Adopt: Require all MS digital communications to use responsive design per WCAG standards.
  • Implement: Complete transition within 6–12 months for newsletters and online publications.
  • Standard: WCAG 2.2 as best‑practice guide for readability and usability.
  • Exceptions: Financial statements or documents solely for printing. If readability matters, make it responsive.
