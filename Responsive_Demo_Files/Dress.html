Club Dress Code

Our attire standards benefit everyone because clothing can elevate or diminish the experience of the room. Our staff takes pride in their appearance and appreciate you respecting our guidelines.

Sand Bar

  • Shirts/cover-ups and proper footwear must be worn at both the interior and exterior dining areas and bars.
  • Tee shirts must be in good condition and not have inappropriate print on them.
  • Denim of any color must be appropriately appointed and free of frayed ends, holes, tears, and patches or fading.
  • Hats are permitted inside the dining area.

Clubhouse Dining & Common Areas (before 5:30pm)

  • Blue jeans, denim shorts, skirts, or jackets are not permitted.
  • Clothing with frayed ends, holes, tears, patches, and fading are not permitted.
  • All other denim colors in pristine condition are permitted.
  • Appropriate golf and tennis attire is acceptable.
  • Blue denim, tank tops, tee shirts, swimwear, cover-ups, and cut-off shorts are not appropriate wear in the clubhouse or on the patio.
  • Gentlemen, please remove your hats when entering the clubhouse.

Clubhouse Dining & Common Areas (after 5:30pm)

  • The Country Club Casual dress code permits long pants or dress “Bermuda” shorts, collared shirts, and appropriate footwear for men.
  • Shorts must be no more than 4 inches above the knee and shirttails must be tucked in except if the shirt is designed to be untucked.
  • “Crocs”, flip-flops, beach shoes, basketball shoes, and all-terrain shoes are not considered appropriate footwear.
  • NOTE: Country Club Casual is also the dress code for Sunday brunch.

The Reserve & Sunday Brunch

  • The Country Club dress code permits long pants, collared shirts, “Bermuda” shorts and appropriate footwear for men. Jackets are always welcome.
  • Shorts must be no more than 4 inches above the knee and shirt tails must be tucked in.
  • “Crocs”, flip-flops, beach shoes, basketball shoes, and all-terrain shoes are not considered appropriate footwear.

If the dress code is modified for a special event, details will be included in the event invitation.

Thank you for your cooperation.
