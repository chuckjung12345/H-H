WELCOME

Kathryn Broccoli
Assistant Director of Food & Beverage

Kat is an experienced hospitality manager with strong administrative skills and a proven track record of building relationships with membership and staff. She holds a degree in Humanities and a Paralegal Certificate, complemented by additional studies in general education from Indian River State College.

Kat previously served as a Dining Manager at Harbour Ridge Yacht & Country Club, where she led teams, developed training plans, and enhanced member experiences through positive engagement. Her high-energy and organized approach shines in collaborative environments focused on problem-solving. Prior to that, Kat worked as the Assistant General Manager at District Table in Stuart.

Kat is originally from Ridgefield, Connecticut and now resides in Stuart with her two dogs. A dedicated sports fan, she follows the Jets, Yankees, and Panthers! Before returning to Florida, Kat spent seven years living in Chicago.
